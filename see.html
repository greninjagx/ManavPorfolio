Tropicial rainforests

First, how does a tropical rainforest look like. Tropical rainforests have plants all over the ground some are poisonous some are not. The poisonous ones include castor bean, Yellow oleander, and Gympie. As well as plants of the ground there are also trees. Some of the most common trees in the amazon include. Kapok tree, Rubber tree and Ipê tree. You will normally also find trails. You might think these are trails of people coming to visit here. Though it actually probably is made from elephants. The amazon also has other types of habits and biomes such as grasslands and swamps some from humans. Swamps look like a pond with tall grass and cattail plants and other tall grasses and plants. They put cattle in this area and turn it into pastures. This is how the amazon will look like it you visited this beautiful forest.
