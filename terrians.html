How is the terrian

If you plan to walk inside the amazon here is what you will have to experience. You actually won’t have to experience a lot the terrain is mostly plain. There will also be plenty of meander, oxbows and tiny streams. Some tiny streams can turn into big ponds since all these area get annual floods. Flat plains doesn’t mean it is easy to walk. The Amazon is very muddy. Rain can reach 2,500mm of precipitation every year some can even reach 3,500mm. Every month it averagest about to 200 to 300mm per month. The driest month july is still more than Canada. In Fact the average precipitation in Calgary, Alberta is 100mm monthly. It is a hard walk like this alone. Though there are a lot of trees in the rainforest. These make it really hard to see where you are going and you will have to climb them to get around. You will also have to deal with all the animals. This means there are lots of snakes and animals camouflage in the forest. If you accidentally get bit by a snake you step on it might mean you have to get to hospital which may be far away. This is how the terrain is in the amazon.
