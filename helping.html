What are poeple doing

Next, people are donating to the WWF. The WWF are a NGO that protects wildlife around the world. They do this by supporting and managing protective areas in the amazon.

What can YOU do

Try not to eat cows

First, try to not eat cow. Cows are normally the main reason people buy land on the amazon. If you don’t depend on these produce then you are saving the amazon.

Paper usage

Next, try to reduce paper usage for your home. Doing this will lower the demand of paper if everyone does it and they won’t need to deforestate as much as they do.

Protest

Finally, Protest against the braziliian government. You might think only people from the brazil will have an impact. Though wherever you live in the world if you join in with environmental peaceful protests you will make a difference. These are a few things you can do to protect the rainforests.
