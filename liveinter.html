Brazil nut trees and bees

Next, I would like to talk about brazil nut trees, and bees. Brazil nut trees grow as their name suggests nuts they also grow flowers. They need pollinators to transfer this to other parts of the tree.. This is where bees come in the bees want to get the nectar inside the flower as doing so they get pollen all over them. When the bee goes to other flowers the pollen shakes of the bee gets a meal and the flowers get fertilizer.

The aguati and the brazil nut tree

Lastly, The aguati and the brazil nut tree. The aguati is just like a squirrel and it takes the nut from the brazil nut tree and eats them. Though the ones that he can’t finish in one meal he will hide in the ground. This seeds with germinate over some time if the aguati doesn’t come back and therefore planting a tree. These are how some living things in the amazon interact.
