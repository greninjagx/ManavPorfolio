Native Culture paragraph/ essay

Information

After doing a prentation on the Haudenosaunee we were asked to make a essay on them. In this essay I have talked about many scientific concepts which will have helped them survive the the harsh enviorment.

Why I choose this

  • Sustainable Forest Management: The text discusses the Haudenosaunee's practices of selectively harvesting trees and conducting controlled burns, which are methods used to maintain the health and diversity of forest ecosystems.
  • Ecosystem Health and Sustainability: The essay emphasizes the Haudenosaunee's understanding of the importance of preserving ecosystem health and sustainability for future generations. This concept is fundamental to environmental science and ecology.
  • Balance with Nature: The text repeatedly refers to the Haudenosaunee's commitment to living in balance with nature, which is a key principle in ecology and environmental science. It implies an understanding of the interconnectedness of all elements within an ecosystem and the importance of maintaining these connections to support biodiversity and ecosystem health.
